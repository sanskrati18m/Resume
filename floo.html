Floo

CompanionFinder TravelTogether

FLOO is a secure travel companion platform that helps users find verified co-travelers for safer journeys. Built with a focus on trust, it uses government ID verification and real-time coordination to enhance travel safety and social connection.

Mission

To make travel safer and more social by helping users connect with verified co-travelers through a secure and seamless platform. .

Built on

Figma

So you effortlessly...

Find trusted companions, feel safer on every journey, and experience travel with confidence.

Key Features Designed:

  • Verified Co-Travelers: Users can find and connect with government-verified companions for safer trips.
  • Real-Time Trip Syncing: Enables travelers to share live travel status and meet-up points.
  • Multi-Role Access: Designed flows for users, verifiers, and travel safety officials.
  • Trust Layer: ID verification, emergency SOS, and reporting options.
  • Flexible Travel Filters: Search by destination, travel date, gender preference, or travel type.

Design Goals:

  • Reduce travel anxiety by emphasizing safety and control.
  • Keep the UI intuitive and mobile-first for on-the-go planning.
  • Build user trust through secure, verified interactions and clear actions.
  • Encourage meaningful social interactions through optional profile details and shared interests.
  • Maintain clarity and focus in high-stress situations with minimal distractions and clear CTAs.
