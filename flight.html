Flight-Booking App

Travel UX Air Tickets

A seamless flight booking app that helps users search, compare, and book flights instantly with real-time prices, secure payments, and instant e-tickets — all in one intuitive platform.

Mission

To simplify air travel by helping users discover, compare, and book flights effortlessly through a secure, user-friendly platform.

Built on

Figma

So you effortlessly...

Book the best flights at the best prices, enjoy a seamless booking experience, and travel with ease and confidence from takeoff to landing.

Key Features Designed:

  • Smart Flight Search: Effortlessly discover the best flight options based on budget, duration, layovers, and more.
  • Real-Time Fare Tracking: Get notified when prices drop or deals are available for your preferred routes.
  • Easy Booking Flow: Streamlined, step-by-step process to book flights quickly without confusion.
  • User Profiles: Save preferences, passenger details, and frequent flyer info for one-tap future bookings.
  • Multiple User Modes: Optimized for solo travelers, families, or corporate users with distinct UX flows.

Design Goals:

  • Simplify the booking journey to reduce user effort and friction.
  • Ensure responsiveness across devices for smooth access on mobile or desktop.
  • Build trust through intuitive UX and transparent pricing.
  • Offer personalization based on past travel behaviors and saved preferences.
